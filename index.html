Professional Background

A Lifetime in Aviation

With four decades of active flying experience, my perspective encompasses every angle of the aviation industry. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Aviation Engineering from The Ohio State University have served as a Multi-Engine Flight Instructor (MEI) and King Air pilot.

My technical foundation was built as an aircraft electrician in the United States Marine Corps. Today, I hold an A&P certificate and am FAA-approved to maintain critical NAS infrastructure, including multiple NAVAIDs, Instrument Landing Systems (ILS), Runway Visual Range (RVR), and AWOS weather systems.

Engineering & PCB Design

Currently, I specialize in aviation-centric electronic systems design, combining my operational flight and aircraft experience with a deep understanding of the current industry needs.

  • Custom PCB Design: Schematic capture and board layout utilizing industry-standard tools like KiCad.
  • Mechanical & 3D Modeling: Custom enclosure and component design.
  • Avionics Communications: Implementation and testing of in-flight connectivity, avionics data buses, including ARINC 429 protocols.
  • System Building & Testing: From custom embedded flight data recorders to rigorous bench testing and troubleshooting of physical hardware.
